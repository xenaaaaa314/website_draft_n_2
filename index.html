ORAMA TECHNOLOGIES

MIXED REALITY COMBAT

Orama Technologies is redefining tactical training through AI-powered mixed reality. We're a team of veterans, engineers, and technologists building the most immersive combat simulation platform on the planet. Our goal is simple: make training more realistic, more adaptive, and more accessible —because lives depend on it.

At Orama we build systems that prepare warfighters, law enforcement, and allied forces for the realities of modern conflict - not outdated scenarios from a different era.

Our Product

Fact 1: Live Fire Fidelity, Zero Risk.

With full-body motion capture, 1:1 weapon replicas, and real-time physics in a 360° MR environment, Orama replicates live-fire CQB with none of the danger.

Fact 2: Adaptive AI Opponents.

Train against near-peer enemies that think and fight like real adversaries, powered by AI that learns and evolves from every session.

Fact 3: Field-Deployable Anywhere

The entire system fits in a Pelican case. Run missions in a FOB tent, police training center, or SOCOM experiment—no internet required.

Fact 4: Real-Time AAR.

Our platform captures every round, decision, and movement, generating real-time After-Action Reviews (AARs) to improve readiness instantly.

Smarter Than Simulation. Orama’s platform uses three integrated AI engines to deliver an unmatched training experience. With Orama, artificial intelligence isn’t just reactive—it’s adaptive, realistic, and mission-driven.
AI Model #1: Computer Vision for Near-Peer Enemies

Real-time tracking of player movement and environmental interaction feeds an adversary AI trained on real-world near-peer tactics.

AI Model #2: AAR for Soldiers & Drones

Our After-Action Review engine analyzes decisions, patterns, and performance to help instructors and trainees instantly identify strengths and weaknesses.

AI Model #3: Voice Escalation & De-Escalation

Operators interact with NPCs using natural language. Our voice-interaction AI supports escalation/de-escalation and shoot/no-shoot decisions based on tone, command, and context.

Our Story

Orama was founded by a veteran who saw the consequences of outdated training firsthand. In modern warfare, seconds and decisions matter—and yet we continue to train with 20th-century tools for 21st-century threats. We knew there had to be a better way. What began as a prototype in a garage is now a system trusted by Tier 1 operators, tested with the USAF, and showcased at national defense conferences. We are not just building a product - we are changing the way America trains for war.

Our Mission

To save lives by making warfighters more prepared, more capable, and more resilient through immersive, intelligent training systems. To close the gap between doctrine and deployment. To outpace our adversaries one simulation at a time.
